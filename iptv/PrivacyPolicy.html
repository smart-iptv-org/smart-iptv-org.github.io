We may collect certain information about your device and usage of the App. This information is collected to help us improve the App and provide you with a better user experience. The types of usage information we may collect include:

1.3 Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics

We use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics, provided by Google, to collect and analyze usage information. Firebase Analytics helps us understand how users interact with the App, while Crashlytics provides us with crash reports to identify and address any technical issues. The information collected by Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics is governed by Google's privacy policy. You can review Google's privacy policy here.

1.4 Firestore Storage

We utilize Firestore Storage, provided by Google, to store sample URLs related to the App's functionalities. This information is stored securely and is not linked to any personal identifiers. Google's privacy policy governs the use of Firestore Storage. You can review Google's privacy policy here.
